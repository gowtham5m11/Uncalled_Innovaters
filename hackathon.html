INNOVATION ISLAND is a dynamic 24-hour hackathon hosted at RVR & JC College of Engineering, bringing together 70 selected teams from colleges across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. The hackathon focuses on solving real-life industrial problems, offering students a unique opportunity to bridge the gap between academia and industry. Participants will engage in a qualifying quiz round and then dive into the hackathon where problem statements collected from industries are given to develop innovative ideas, designs, and prototypes. The event not only challenges participants' technical skills but also encourages creativity, teamwork, and networking with industry professionals. The solutions generated during the hackathon have the potential for real-world impact, as they will be further taken to industries for implementation, making UNCALLED INNOVATORS a platform which young innovative minds with industries
